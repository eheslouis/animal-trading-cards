Shih Tzu

A shih tzu also known as the Chrysanthemum Dog, is a toy dog breed, weighing 10 - 12 pounds when full grown, with long silky hair

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 7.9 - 11in.
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 16 years
  • Habitat: companion animal

The exact origins of the breed are unknown, but it is thought to have originated in Tibet and then been developed in China although various hypotheses exist.
